MavHub Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are our highest priority.

Introduction

Welcome to MavHub (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the MavHub platform.

Information We Collect

1. User-Provided Content

MavHub allows you to create and share content, including but not limited to posts, comments, images, and profile pictures (“User Content”). Any User Content you provide is stored securely on our servers and is accessible only to you and those you choose to share it with (such as followers or the public, based on your privacy settings).

2. Account Information

To create an account, you may be required to provide information such as your username, email address, and password. This information is used solely to maintain your account and provide access to MavHub’s features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve MavHub’s features.
  • Personalize your experience, such as displaying your profile picture or recommending relevant content.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, support, or promotional materials (when you have opted in).
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve user experience and site performance.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating MavHub (e.g., hosting or analytics), but only under strict confidentiality obligations that comply with applicable privacy laws.

Data Security and Storage

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. This includes using secure servers, encryption, and routine security assessments. While we strive to protect your personal information, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by accessing your account settings. If you wish to fully delete your account or have any questions about your data, please contact us at sjsmavhub@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

MavHub is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information.

Policy Updates

We may update or revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any modifications on this page and update the “Last Updated” date below.
